PageHawk.io

Webpage content analytics

Pagehawk.io is designed to provide quick webpage analytics like; word count, sentence count, flesch readabilty score, read time, response time, keyword density, summary, headings, outbound links.

All the user needs to do is provide a valid url and analyses is done.

This is meant to be a portfolio project, and show use of full stack web development.
